## Tris (1 M, pH 7.5)

Tris recipe for buffering *V. fischeri* growth media.

Amount | Reagent
------:|:-------
127 g  | Tris-HCl
23.6 g | Tris base

Stir until dissolved, adjust to pH 7.5, then adjust volume to 1L with dI H<sub>2</sub>O.

Filter-sterilize and store at room temperature.
